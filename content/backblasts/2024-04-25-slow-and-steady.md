**The Thang:**

- Two groups - 3 repeats of 3 rounds
- First round - First group bear crawls to second light post then lunges back, and second group does 10 second pushups (10 s down then up normal)
- Second round - First group caterpillar to second light post then jump squat back, and second group does 10 second curl (5 s down and 5 s up)
- Third round - First group bear crawls to the second light post then lunges back, and second group does burpees with 5 second pushup (5 s down and 5 s up)
- Light post group, plank until your whole group finishes
