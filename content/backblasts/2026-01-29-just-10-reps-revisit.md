**Where:** #ao-ad-astra

**Q:** @Waco
**PAX:** @Wreck It, @404, @Dizzy, @Farmers Only, @Bonnet, @Big Toe
**Count:** 7

**Warmup (~5 min)**
- SSH Burn Down - 7 ↓
- Arm Circles
- Piano Man

Repeat Movement round and Slow Motion round until time.

**Movement Round (~5 min)**
- Farmer / Bear Crawl Team
    - Pair up
    - One PAX bear crawls, while the other farmer carries both blocks along side.
    - When either PAX needs to stop/put down blocks, both PAX:
    - Do 10 Merkins
    - Switch and keep going to time

**Slow Motion - 10 Reps**
- Using a metronome - set to 4 beats per count, approx 55 BPM
- 10 reps of each exercise, with a 4 count in one direction, 4 count in the other for 1 (ONE)
    - Example: Heavy Squat - Down, 2, 3, 4, Up 2, 3, 4 = ONE
    - The goal is slow, no momentum movement. Focus on the burn
    - 1 rep - ~8 beats X 10 reps = 80 beats ~ 1m 30 seconds per exercise
- Exercise Round (~9 min)
    - OH Press
    - Curls
    - Row
    - Dead Lift
    - Heavy Squat
    - Dry Docks
