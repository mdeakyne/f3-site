I’m the only one who can do it the way it needs to be done. It’ll be quicker if I just do it myself. Sound familiar?In our world where efficiency is the top priority, what effect does that have on our formation as humans? For loneliness? For pride? Going it alone may seem safer and more efficient, but it will have damaging effects long term. Going together will be slower and more risky (when you involve other people they can fail you and you might have to forgive someone), but you will have the character, perseverance, and encouragement to go far together.How does this apply to family? Marriage? Parenting? Work? Your worshiping community?
