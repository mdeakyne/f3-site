**Backblast: Week O' Waco (WoW) Part 1 - Greatest Hits (Remix)**
**Where:** #ao-beehive
**When:** 3/31/26 @0530
**Q:** @Waco
**PAX:** @Farmers Only @Big Toe @Toto @404 @Casio @Bonnet

**Warmup:**
- Arm Circles
- Piano Man

**The Thang:**
Mashup of some of Waco's favorite hits, Surfs Up, Ladders, and 20% rest.

🏄 **Surfs Up - 12 minutes**
PAX form a tight circle, facing inward. Each PAX will hold the "worst" or hardest position of the current exercise (as called by the Q). The Q sets the tone and tempo — the wave starts with him. 1's, then 2's, then 3's and around the wave goes:
- OH Press
- Row
- Squats
- Merkins

🪜 **Movement Ladders - 12 minutes**
3 run ladder, moving exercise out to the first cone (rung), jog back, and then out to the next, out to the last, repeat. Loop all for four exercises 'till time.
- Bear Crawl Out - Jog Back
- Lunges Out - Jog Back
- Murder Bunny Out - Rifle Carry Back
- Thread the Needle Out - Jog Back

⏱ **20% Rest - 15 minutes (3 sets)**
60 seconds per exercise, AMRAP, x4, then 60 seconds of rest:
- Set 1 - OH Press, Curls, Row, Dead lift
- Set 2 - Heavy Squats, Lunges, SSH, Calf Raises
- Set 3 - Flutter, Heels to Heaven, Heavy Freddy, XYs.
