### The Thang: Laddered Stations

6 stations, 6 exercises:

1. 10 Burpees
2. 15 Heavy Squats
3. 20 Overhead press
4. 25 Heavy Lunges
5. 30 Merkins
6. 35 Tricep dips

Instructions:

- 1st station: do the first exercise
- 2nd station: do the first 2 exercises
- 3rd station: first 3 exercises
- Continue up to doing all 6 at the 6th station
- Murder bunny between stations
- After the 6th, work back down:
    - 5th station: last 5 exercises
    - 4th station: last 4 exercises
    - Continue until doing just dips at the 1st station

### COT

Working through a period where feels like work is only being added, and ready for the part where some things start to drop off (and ready to put this cold gear away).
