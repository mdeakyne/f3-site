COT: Slowly in the right directionThe right kind of growth doesn’t come quickly. Like in the parable of the sower, the seed that falls on the rocks grows quickly, but doesn’t have the roots to make it through the heat of the day. Lasting growth occurs over time as you move slowly in the right direction. We are “being transformed from one degree of glory to another” (2 Cor 3:16-18). While we are responsible to put in the work to grow, it is God who brings the growth in our life. In all spheres of our lives (spiritually, physically, vocationally, with family, with friends, with our worshipping community), we are both active and dependent.
