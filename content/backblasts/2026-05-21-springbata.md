**Where:** #ao-ad-astra

**Q:** @404
**PAX:** @Waco @casio @Big Toe @Dizzy

Warmup
Burndown jumping jacks from 5
Arm circles

The thang: Tabata stations

8 stations:
Scissor lift
Hamstring Curls (Feet on a yoga ball. Lift your pelvis off the ground similar to glute bridges, then roll the ball in and out)
Single dumbell clean and push jerk
Hodor
Decline Pushup (Feet on a block on its end or on its side)
Heavy Calf Raise
Tricep Dip
Deadlift

Divide up on stations. Each pax performs the exercise at the station for 1/2 of a tabata song, then rotate. Completed two passes through each station.
