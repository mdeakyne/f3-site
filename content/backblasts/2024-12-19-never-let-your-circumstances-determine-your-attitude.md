Cot:  I was reminded that you should never let you circumstances determine your attitude when I came across a podcast, “48 Days to the Work You Love” came up.  I hadn’t listened to it in a while.  I forgot that the host pasted away and it was his last podcast.  He said that he had stage 4 cancer and was given 3 months to live.  However he is using that time to spend time with family and see friends.  He can’t control the cancer but can use the time he has left.  As for me, my Mom is in the nursing home and while I can’t control her dementia, i can control the time I spend with her.   Sometime there are things we can’t control but we can control our attitude on how we handle them.    (edited)
