Warmup:
Indian Run around the loop

The Thang:
First Loop:
10 rounds of:

- 10 OP
- 10 Curls
- 10 Rows
- 10 Heavy Squats

While partner jogs the loop. First team of 2 to finish picks the next 4 exercises.

Second Loop:
10 rounds of:

- 10 Blockees
- 10 LBC
- 10 Dead Bugs
- 10 Cossack Squats

Took us right up to time.

CoT: 
My attitude last Thursday was bad. If you don't like a workout, don't complain in the moment—volunteer to lead instead. Change things, try new approaches, and see what works. We get better as a group this way.

3 improvements I made from last Thursday:

- Crankable (Simpler exercises, fewer compound movements)
- Bankable (Transfer current count to partner so no one misses counts)
- Rankable (Work toward a goal—winners choose exercises)
