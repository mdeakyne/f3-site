**COT:**“Why tho?” This is the most important question we ask. Why tho? Get up at 5am to sweat on a cinder block, why tho? Trade that beer for a water, why tho? Get off my phone to talk to my wife, why tho? Our ***why*** is where we find all the ***power***. If we don’t know why we do what we do, we won’t make ***high-value decisions*** with any kind of consistency. Especially when the alternative skews towards instant gratification. Ask ourselves, ask each other, and ask God this question regularly. And don’t tolerate shit answers from yourself or your brother. Seek the best, don’t settle for less. That’s what we were created for, because God said so. That’s why tho.**Coffeeteria**: Yes
