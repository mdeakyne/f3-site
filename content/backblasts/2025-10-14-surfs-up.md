**Where:** [#ao-beehive](https://f3lawrence.slack.com/archives/C07A8STLZ5Z)

**Q:** [@Waco](https://f3lawrence.slack.com/team/U0727KRQT5J)
**PAX:** :wreck-it-ralph:, [@404](https://f3lawrence.slack.com/team/U07G685DW2K), [@Big Toe](https://f3lawrence.slack.com/team/U05LQM40U4R), [@Dizzy](https://f3lawrence.slack.com/team/U08N6V1HF32), [@Farmers Only](https://f3lawrence.slack.com/team/U05NHFL431A), @dialup
**Count:** 7

**Format:** Circle of Pain
**Type:** Endurance / Core / Strength
**Concept:** Ride the Wave

**The Setup**
PAX form a tight circle, facing inward. Each PAX holds the hardest position of the current exercise (as called by the Q). The Q sets the tone and tempo—the **wave** starts with him.

**The Flow**

1. **Q starts the wave:** Performs **1 rep** of the current exercise while everyone else holds the static position.
2. The wave moves **clockwise**—each PAX performs their 1 rep while others continue to hold.
3. Once it returns to the Q, he performs **2 reps**.
4. The wave continues—each PAX now performs 2 reps when it's their turn.
5. The count climbs each time the wave returns to the Q (3, 4, 5, etc.) until Q calls time or the set ends.

**The Catch**

- Everyone holds the hardest position while waiting.
- The goal is to "ride the wave"—stay strong through the hold, stay focused during your reps.
- When fatigue hits, it's all about teamwork: everyone feels the same burn, together.

**Strength**

- OH Press
- Thrusters
- Curls (hold OH Press)
- Upright Row (hold up)
- Squats (heavy)
- Blockees

**Core**

- Russian Hammer
- Heavy Freddy's (4 count)
- Flutter Kick (4 count)
- Heels to Heaven
- Glute Bridge (heavy)
- XY's (4 count)

5 reps—one round.
