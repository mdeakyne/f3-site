*The Setup*
Use a baseball diamond or set 4 cones roughly 90 feet apart to form a square. PAX begin at home plate.
The workout follows a "7 of Diamonds" structure — reps increase as you move toward the center of the diamond, then descend back down the opposite side.

*The Process*
1. Start at home plate.
2. Travel to the next cone/base and complete the assigned reps and excercise
3. Continue around the diamond, repeating the same reps at each base until returning home.
4. Hold a plank for the six before beginning the next movement.
5. Move up to the next level or reps and excercise

*The Catch*
PAX must always face south during movement between cones:
• Run forward
• Side shuffle
• Backpedal
• Side shuffle

This creates constant directional changes and keeps the legs under tension throughout the workout.
